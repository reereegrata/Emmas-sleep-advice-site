About Emma

An Australian mum on a mission to help parents and their bubs sleep better — one honest guide at a time.

👩

Hi, I'm Emma 👋

I'm an Australian mum who's been through the sleep struggle firsthand — the 2am wake-ups, the conflicting advice from every direction, and the guilt when nothing seems to work.

After my own bub struggled with sleep for months, I realised there was no single place for Australian parents to get honest, practical, and research-backed advice without the fluff. So I created it.

Emma's Sleep Advice is my way of sharing what I've learned — and what actually works — with other families who deserve a good night's rest as much as their bubs do.

🔬

Evidence-Based

Every guide and review is backed by research, expert recommendations, or real-world testing — never guesswork.

💜

Honest Reviews

I only recommend products I've personally tested or genuinely trust. No sponsored fluff, no fake endorsements.

🇦🇺

Australian Made

All content is tailored for Australian families — AU standards, AU brands, and advice that works in our context.

💜 My Mission

To help every Australian parent feel confident, supported, and well-rested — by providing the honest, practical sleep advice I wish I'd had from day one.

📖 Emma's Story

2023

Had my first bub. Spent months navigating sleepless nights, contradictory advice, and endless product research. Felt overwhelmed and alone.

2024

Started sharing what I learned with other mums — sleep schedules, product recommendations, gentle routines. Realised how much Australian parents needed a trusted resource.

2025

Decided to build Emma's Sleep Advice — a home for honest, research-backed sleep guidance made specifically for Australian families. No judgment, no fluff, just real help.

2026

Launched the site. Now helping hundreds of Australian parents get the sleep advice they deserve — and growing every week. 🚀
