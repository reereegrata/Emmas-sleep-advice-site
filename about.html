About Emma

I'm a mum on a mission.

After my first, I tested every swaddle, mattress, baby monitor, and white noise machine I could find — because I refused to believe "some babies just don't sleep."

I'm not a doctor or a certified sleep consultant. I'm just a real mum who spent way too many nights doom-scrolling Google at 3am, trying to figure out why my baby wouldn't sleep — and what actually worked.

Emma's Sleep Advice is where I share everything I learned: honest product comparisons, evidence-based tips, and practical tools that actually make a difference. No fluff, no sponsored nonsense — just what works.

How It Started

When my first baby arrived, I hit the same wall every new parent hits: sleep deprivation so intense you forget what a full night feels like. I tried every gadget, every technique, every "magic" solution I could find online. Some worked. Most didn't.

What I discovered was that there's no shortage of sleep advice online — just a shortage of honest, practical advice. Most reviews are sponsored. Most guides are written by people who've never changed a nappy at 2am.

So I started testing products myself. Reading the research. Talking to other Aussie mums. And slowly, I built a system that worked for my family — and started sharing it with friends. Word spread, and Emma's Sleep Advice was born.
