Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how this site earns and why we only recommend products we trust.

💜 The short version: Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click and buy, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in and would use with our own bub. Your trust matters more than any commission.

What Are Affiliate Links?

An affiliate link is a special URL that tracks when someone clicks from our site to a retailer (like Amazon AU) and makes a purchase. If you buy something after clicking our link, the retailer gives us a small percentage of the sale — at absolutely no extra cost to you.

How We Use Affiliate Links

Emma's Sleep Advice participates in affiliate programs, including:

  • Amazon Australia Associates Program — As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.
  • Other affiliate programs may be added over time. This page will be updated accordingly.

Does This Affect Our Reviews?

No. Never.

Every product we review is tested and evaluated honestly. The presence (or absence) of an affiliate program has zero influence on our recommendations. If a product isn't good enough, we say so — regardless of whether we could earn a commission from it.

We will never recommend a product just because we can earn from it. That would betray the trust of every Australian parent who visits this site — and that trust is worth far more than any commission.

How We Choose Products to Review

Products are selected based on:

  • 🔬 Research — Is it backed by evidence and safety standards?
  • 🛏️ Real-world testing — Have we (or parents we trust) actually used it?
  • 🇦🇺 Australian relevance — Is it available and suitable for Aussie families?
  • 💬 Parent feedback — What are other mums and dads saying?

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running this site takes time and money — researching products, writing in-depth comparisons, and maintaining the website. Affiliate commissions help cover those costs so Emma's Sleep Advice can stay free for everyone.

We'll never charge you to access our content. No paywalls. No subscriptions. Just honest advice, supported by affiliate earnings.

Your Choice

You are never obligated to use our affiliate links. If you prefer to go directly to a retailer's website, that's completely fine. Our goal is to help you make informed decisions — whether you use our links or not.
