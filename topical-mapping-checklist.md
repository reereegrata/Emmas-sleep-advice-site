## What Happens When We Add New Products?

**Scenario:** May bagong product category (e.g., baby monitor with new tech, or a new AU brand).

**Hindi na kailangan ng full re-map.** Quick check lang:

1. **Trends test** — may search demand ba for "best [new product] australia"?
2. **SERP check** — ano format ng top results?
3. **Existing cluster audit** — may pillar page ba tayo na pwedeng i-expand?
   - If yes: update pillar page (add product, adjust comparison table)
   - If no: new pillar page for new cluster
4. **Internal links** — connect new/updated page to existing guides
5. **Add to topical map** — update yung entity section
6. **Update STATUS.md and push**

Hindi kailangan ulitin yung buong Steps 1-5 para sa isang bagong product. Yung nap lang natin as the site grows, we add to it incrementally. Yung big mapping effort na ito ay para mabuo yung foundation — after that, small updates lang per new product.

- Competitor gaps = opportunity, not guesswork
