Questions from Australian Parents

Answered by Emma, based on real experience and research.

When should I start sleep training? +
Most experts recommend waiting until your bub is 4-6 months old. Every baby is different — I cover the signs of readiness in my Newborn Sleep Schedule guide.
How do I know which cot mattress is safe? +
In Australia, look for the AS/NZS 8811.1:2013 standard. I test all mattresses against this in my Best Baby Mattresses guide.
Are white noise machines safe for newborns? +
Yes, when used correctly — keep it at a safe volume (under 50dB) and place it at least 1m from your bub's cot. I've tested the top Australian models.
